Formula Reference Cards

Three Phase Current
I = P / (√3 × V × PF)
Where P = Power (W), V = Voltage (V), PF = Power Factor
Motor Current
I = P / (√3 × V × PF × η)
Where η = Motor Efficiency
Transformer kVA
kVA = (√3 × V × I) / 1000
For three-phase transformer sizing
Voltage Drop
VD = √3 × I × R × L
Where R = resistance (Ω/m), L = cable length (m)
Power Factor Correction
Q = P(tan φ₁ − tan φ₂)
Reactive power required for PF correction (kVAR)
Short Circuit Current
Isc = V / (√3 × Z)
Where Z = system impedance (Ω)
Demand Load
MD = Σ(P × DF)
Where DF = Demand Factor per load type
Generator Sizing
G = (MD × 1.25) / PF
Generator kVA with 25% safety margin
Capacitor Bank
C = P × (tan φ₁ − tan φ₂)
Capacitor bank size in kVAR
Earth Fault Loop
Zs = Ze + (R1 + R2)
Total earth fault loop impedance
Diversity Factor
DF = MD / (Σ Connected Load)
Ratio of actual maximum demand to installed load
Maximum Demand
MD = Σ(kW × LUF × DF)
Load Utilization Factor × Demand Factor per circuit

ETAP Studies

ETAP Power System Analysis

Comprehensive power system analysis studies using ETAP software for UAE construction and industrial projects.

Load Flow Study

Analysis of voltage profiles, power flows, and loading conditions under normal and emergency operating conditions. Verifies DEWA voltage drop limits.

Voltage Profile Power Flow Cable Loading

Short Circuit Study

3-phase, single-phase, and line-to-line fault current calculations. Used for breaker sizing, busbar rating, and cable withstand verification per IEC 60909.

Fault Levels Breaker Sizing IEC 60909

Motor Starting Study

Analysis of voltage dip during motor starting. Critical for large motors (>75kW). Evaluates DOL, Star-Delta, VFD, and soft-starter options.

Voltage Dip Starting Method VFD Analysis

Arc Flash Study

Incident energy analysis per IEEE 1584. Determines arc flash boundaries, PPE requirements, and safe working distances for electrical panels.

IEEE 1584 PPE Level Safety Zones

Protection Coordination

Time-current curve coordination between fuses, MCCBs, and relays. Ensures selective operation — only the faulty section is isolated while healthy circuits remain energized.

TCC Curves Selectivity Relay Settings

Harmonic Analysis

Total harmonic distortion (THD) analysis for VFD, UPS, and non-linear loads. Identifies resonance risks and filter requirements per IEEE 519.

THD Analysis IEEE 519 Filter Design
Fire Alarm Systems

Fire & Life Safety Design

Complete fire alarm system design expertise including addressable, conventional systems, and Civil Defence submission requirements.

Addressable Systems

• Individual device identification per loop

• Apollo XP95 / Discovery protocols

• Hochiki ESP protocol compatible

• System Sensor intelligent detectors

• Up to 200 devices per loop

• Fault isolation via short circuit isolators

• Civil Defence compliant zoning

• Graphical mimic display capability

Conventional Systems

• Zone-based detection (not device level)

• Suitable for small/medium buildings

• Lower cost vs addressable

• Maximum 20–32 devices per zone

• 2-wire and 4-wire configurations

• End-of-line resistors required

• Civil Defence approved panels
